About Us

We aim to make student loans quick and hassle free for Indians many students especially in tier 2 and tier 3 cities are unaware of the fact that they can get an education loan without collateral and they need not visit any bank for that, We at Epicred not only make the entire process easy but also negotiate the best deal for students from the lender without charging them anything , We want to make sure that no student should give up hope on getting quality education due to financial limitation.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, our first office was a park in sector 34, where we did tele calling to Immigration consultants for potential clients and later on visited consultants across states to build our network. We have been profitable from the beginning having disbursed loans over 8 crores in our first four months.

Plan for 2023

2023 is going to be a breakthrough year for us, we are going to create an online marketplace and increase our social media presence. We are targeting disbursals over 60 crore rupees in 2023.To achieve our target we are increasing our lending partners and intend to focus on students planning their studies in the United States.
